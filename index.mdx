Fontcy is a free, open-source fancy text generator that transforms your plain text into Unicode-styled characters you can use anywhere — social media bios, usernames, posts, and more. Just type, pick a style, and copy.

## Get started in seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Fontcy">
    Visit [Fontcy](https://github.com/b1naryn1nja/fontcy) and open `index.html` in your browser, or use a hosted version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your text">
    Enter any text in the input box at the top. Fontcy instantly previews it in all 18 font styles as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a style">
    Browse the grid of font previews and find the style you want — Script, Bold Sans, Fraktur, Bubble, and many more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to copy">
    Click any font box to copy the styled Unicode text to your clipboard. Paste it anywhere.
  </Step>
</Steps>
